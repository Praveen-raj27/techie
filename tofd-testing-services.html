At Techie Training and Inspections, we specialize in Time of Flight Diffraction (TOFD) testing services, an advanced Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) technique renowned for its unparalleled accuracy in flaw detection and sizing. TOFD is a highly efficient ultrasonic method that uses the diffraction of ultrasonic waves from the edges of discontinuities, providing precise information about defect location and size.

Why Choose TOFD Testing?

TOFD stands out for its ability to detect and size cracks and other flaws with incredible precision. Unlike conventional ultrasonic methods, TOFD excels in high-speed inspections while offering comprehensive coverage of the inspection area.

Our TOFD Services Include:

  • Weld Inspection:  Advanced evaluation of welds to detect cracks, lack of fusion, and other discontinuities.
  • Flaw Detection:  Precise identification and sizing of defects in metals and other materials.
  • Thickness Measurement:  Accurate assessment of material thickness for corrosion monitoring.
  • Integrity Assessments:  Ensuring the reliability of critical assets in high-pressure and high-stress environments.

Industries We Serve:

  • Windmill Towers:   Ensuring weld integrity and structural soundness of windmill components.
  • Pressure Vessels:  High-precision inspection to detect cracks and other flaws in pressure-bearing equipment.
  • Shipping Industry:  Detecting defects in ship hulls, bulkheads, and structural welds.
  • Oil and Gas:  Pipeline and storage tank inspections to ensure safe operations.
  • Power Plants:  Examining boilers, turbines, and high-pressure components for critical defects.
  • Heavy Structural Fabrication:  Verifying weld quality in large-scale structures.
  • Manufacturing and Fabrication:  Inspection of newly fabricated components to ensure compliance with standards.

Key Features of TOFD Testing:

  • High Sensitivity:   Capable of detecting even minute cracks and defects.
  • Accurate Defect Sizing:  Provides precise measurements of defect dimensions.
  • Comprehensive Coverage:  Scans the entire weld volume, leaving no area unchecked.
  • High-Speed Inspection:  Faster than many other NDT methods without compromising accuracy.
  • Data Reliability:  TOFD results are repeatable and offer clear imaging of defects.

Applications of TOFD:

  • Weld inspections in critical infrastructure.
  • Crack detection in pipelines and pressure vessels.
  • Corrosion monitoring and material degradation assessment.
  • Integrity assessment of high-stress components.
  • Inspection of complex geometries and inaccessible areas.
