Custom dimensions can be provided based on specific requirements for different PAUT/TFM applications.

Description

Our Micro Cracks Demonstration Blocks are meticulously designed to replicate minute, hard-to-detect cracks in various materials, typically encountered in high-stress environments or fatigue conditions. These blocks are ideal for training NDT technicians, calibrating equipment, and validating inspection procedures, especially for detecting very small or subsurface defects.
Material Composition: Manufactured from carbon steel, stainless steel, or other materials that are relevant to your industry, ensuring a realistic representation of the components and structures being tested in real-world applications.
Defect Simulation: Designed with a variety of defect types, including cracks, porosity, lack of fusion, and other discontinuities, these blocks provide comprehensive training for both surface and subsurface defect detection.
Total Focusing Method Ready: Specifically created to maximize the benefits of TFM technology, these blocks allow for the highest resolution image reconstruction possible, helping technicians understand and interpret complex defect profiles.
