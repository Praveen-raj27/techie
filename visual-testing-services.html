At Techie Training and Inspections, we provide Visual Testing (VT) services to help clients ensure the structural integrity and safety of their components and assets. Visual Testing is one of the most fundamental and effective Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) methods, designed to detect surface-level defects quickly and cost-effectively.

Why Visual Testing (VT)?

Visual Testing is an essential first-line inspection method used to identify visible defects such as cracks, corrosion, misalignments, weld discontinuities, and other surface irregularities. With the aid of advanced tools like magnifiers, boroscopes, and cameras, VT provides reliable insights into the condition of components, ensuring operational safety and performance.

Our VT Services Include:

  • Surface Defect Detection:  Identifying cracks, wear, and other visible defects.
  • Weld Inspection:  Weld Inspection: Ensuring the quality of welds by detecting surface discontinuities.
  • Alignment Checks:  Verifying dimensional accuracy and proper alignment of components.
  • Corrosion Inspection:  Assessing the extent of surface corrosion and damage.
  • Advanced Tools:  Using high-tech equipment for detailed visual examinations.

Industries We Serve:

  • Windmill Towers:   Inspecting critical welds and components to ensure structural safety.
  • Pressure Vessels:  Checking for visible flaws in exterior and interior surfaces.
  • Shipping Industry:  Examining hulls, decks, and structural elements for cracks or corrosion.
  • Heavy Structural Fabrication:  Ensuring surface quality and alignment in large fabrications.
  • Manufacturing and Construction:  Detecting defects in tools, components, and finished structures.
  • Automotive and Aerospace:  Inspecting machined parts and assemblies for surface irregularities.

Benefits of Visual Testing:

  • Non-invasive and quick to perform.
  • Cost-effective solution for routine inspections.
  • Detects critical issues early, preventing costly failures.
  • Can be performed on-site or in-house.
