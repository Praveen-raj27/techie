Eddy Current Testing – REFRESHER PROGRAM

This course is ideal for individuals with a foundational understanding of eddy current inspection techniques who seek intensive instruction to enhance their knowledge and gain hands-on experience in preparation for recertification examinations. It is also suitable for candidates aiming to advance towards higher eddy current examination qualifications. The course features a high practical content, and all necessary equipment is provided.

Special Note:

Previous certification in a Level 1 or Level 2 method is required. This course is not intended as a substitute for standard training courses but serves as an excellent supplementary option to reinforce and expand existing knowledge.

Training Days

Eddy Current Testing (ET) – 5-Days Course for Surface Inspection (40 Hours)

Course Outline

The course covers the following key areas:
  • Revision of basic techniques: Refresh foundational knowledge and skills related to eddy current testing
  • Practical exercises on equipment: Hands-on experience with eddy current testing equipment to reinforce theoretical learning
  • Theory package oriented towards recent updates: A comprehensive theory module covering the latest advancements and updates in eddy current testing practices
  • Relevant codes & standards: Review and apply current codes, standards, and specifications relevant to eddy current testing
  • Instruction writing: Learn how to prepare detailed and clear testing instructions, ensuring accurate execution and reporting
  • Mock examination: Simulate an exam environment to test and consolidate knowledge, preparing for certification or recertification
