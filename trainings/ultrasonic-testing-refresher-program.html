Ultrasonic Testing – REFRESHER PROGRAM

This course is ideal for individuals with a basic understanding of Ultrasonic Testing techniques who seek intensive instruction to enhance their knowledge and gain additional experience in preparation for recertification. It is also suitable for candidates aiming to advance toward higher ultrasonic examination qualifications and features a strong practical component. All necessary equipment will be provided.

Special Note:

Previous certification in a Level 1 or Level 2 Ultrasonic Testing method is required. This course is not a replacement for standard training programs but serves as an excellent supplementary course to further develop your skills.

Training Days

Ultrasonic Testing (UT) – 5-Days Course for Weld Inspection/casting/forging(40 Hours)

Course Outline

The course covers the following key areas:
  • Revision of basic Ultrasonic Testing techniques
  • Probe identification and selection
  • Test sensitivity and calibration
  • Equipment checks and maintenance
  • Geometry and ultrasonic technique selection
  • Defect response and evaluation
  • Practical exercises on a wide range of weldments
  • Methods for sizing defects accurately
