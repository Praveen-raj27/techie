At Techie Training and Inspections, we provide Phased Array Ultrasonic Testing (PAUT) services, an advanced Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) method that redefines precision, efficiency, and versatility in flaw detection and material evaluation. PAUT uses multiple ultrasonic beams, controlled electronically, to scan materials and components with exceptional accuracy, ensuring the integrity and reliability of critical assets.

Why Phased Array Ultrasonic Testing?

PAUT is a highly advanced version of Ultrasonic Testing that enables detailed imaging and volumetric inspections. With its ability to inspect complex geometries, welds, and materials, PAUT is ideal for applications where conventional methods may fall short.

Our PAUT Services Include:

  • Weld Inspection:  Comprehensive analysis of welds to detect cracks, porosity, lack of fusion, and other defects.
  • Corrosion Mapping:  Identifying and quantifying corrosion and erosion in pipelines, storage tanks, and other structures.
  • Flaw Sizing and Characterization:  Accurately determining the size, location, and orientation of defects.

Industries We Serve:

  • Windmill Towers:   Inspecting welds and structural components to ensure operational reliability.
  • Pressure Vessels:  High-accuracy testing of vessels for flaws and defects.
  • Shipping Industry:  Scanning hulls, bulkheads, and welds for internal and surface discontinuities.
  • Oil and Gas:  Pipeline inspection, corrosion mapping, and critical asset assessment.
  • Power Plants:  Evaluating boilers, turbines, and critical components for defects.
  • Heavy Structural Fabrication:  Ensuring weld integrity in large-scale construction projects.
  • Aerospace and Automotive:  Assessing advanced materials and components for safety and reliability.

Applications of PAUT:

  • Inspection of welds in critical structures.
  • Corrosion monitoring in pipelines and tanks.
  • Defect detection in composite and advanced materials.
  • Thickness measurement for structural integrity assessment.
