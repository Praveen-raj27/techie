At Techie Training and Inspections, we specialize in providing Magnetic Particle Testing (MT) services to ensure the structural integrity and reliability of critical components. MT is a trusted and efficient Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) technique used to detect surface and near-surface flaws in ferromagnetic materials.

Why Choose Magnetic Particle Testing (MT)?

MT is ideal for identifying defects such as cracks, seams, laps, or inclusions in components where strength and safety are paramount. It is widely used across industries for its ability to reveal critical flaws that may compromise operational safety and performance

Our MT Services Include:

  • Detection of Surface and Subsurface Defects:  Locating flaws such as fatigue cracks, weld defects, or stress corrosion cracks.
  • Customized Inspections:  Tailored MT solutions to meet specific client requirements.
  • Compliance with Industry Standards:  Adhering to ASME, ISO, AWS, and other international standards.
  • Experienced Technicians:  Certified inspectors with extensive experience in performing precise and efficient MT services.

Industries We Serve:

  • Windmill Towers:   Ensuring weld integrity and structural reliability of renewable energy assets.
  • Pressure Vessels:  Detecting stress and fatigue cracks to maintain safety in critical pressure equipment.
  • Shipping Industry:  Inspecting hulls, decks, and structural elements for corrosion or damage.
  • Heavy Structural Fabrication:  Verifying the quality of welded structures used in construction and industrial applications.
  • Automotive and Manufacturing:  Inspecting machined parts and assemblies for flaws that may affect performance.
