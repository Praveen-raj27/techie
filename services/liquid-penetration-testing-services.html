Why Choose Liquid Penetrant Testing (PT)?

Liquid Penetrant Testing is a simple yet highly effective method for identifying surface defects such as cracks, porosity, and seams in components. It is versatile, non-invasive, and suitable for a wide range of materials, including metals, ceramics, and plastics.
