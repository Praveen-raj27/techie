Description

Our Micro Cracks Demonstration Blocks are meticulously designed to replicate minute, hard-to-detect cracks in various materials, typically encountered in high-stress environments or fatigue conditions. These blocks are ideal for training NDT technicians, calibrating equipment, and validating inspection procedures, especially for detecting very small or subsurface defects.
Material Composition: High-grade materials such as carbon steel, stainless steel, or alloys, selected to simulate real-world micro-cracking behavior in different industrial environments
Defect Simulation: Precisely engineered to represent microscopic cracks, including surface-breaking and subsurface defects, making them perfect for high-resolution testing methods like ultrasonic, phased array, and eddy current testing.
Precision Machining: Manufactured using state-of-the-art techniques to ensure the micro cracks are of accurate size, depth, and orientation for realistic NDT inspection scenarios.
